# App Store Listing Draft

## Name
TallyHo: Budget & Bill Planner

## Subtitle
Weekly budgets without the chaos

## Promotional text
Plan bills, smooth large payments, check whether a purchase fits, and see what is left after every week or payday.

## Description
TallyHo turns a messy month into a clear weekly or biweekly money plan.

Build recurring bills and income, view upcoming payments on a calendar, smooth large expenses with Split Coach, and use “Can I buy it?” for a direct answer before you spend.

Free features include:
- Budget overview and payment calendar
- Weekly cash-flow plan
- Up to 25 planned items
- Three purchase checks per week

TallyHo Premium adds:
- Unlimited budget items
- Weekly and biweekly Split Coach
- Unlimited “Can I buy it?” checks
- Spending and savings insights
- Debt payoff planning

TallyHo is a planning tool and does not connect to or move money from financial accounts in this version.

## Keywords
budget, bills, weekly budget, biweekly budget, spending, savings, debt, paycheck, planner, money
